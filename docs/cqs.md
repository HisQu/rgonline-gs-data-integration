# Competency Questions (Harmonized Data)

1. Which individuals can be reliably identified as the same historical person across DNB, Germania Sacra, and RGO, and where do the sources disagree?
2. For a selected person, how does their activity develop over time and space (dates, places, and documented contexts)?
3. Which names, name variants, and titles are used for the same person, and what do these variations suggest about status, role, or regional usage?
4. In which regesta and subentries does a person appear, and what concrete documentary evidence supports their attributed actions or positions?
5. Which people, places, and institutions cluster together around the same periods, indicating possible networks, conflicts, or political-ecclesiastical connections?
